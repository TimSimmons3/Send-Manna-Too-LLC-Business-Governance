# Send Manna Too LLC Business Governance Framework
## Release 0.1 Development Plan

Document status: Draft  
Version: 0.1  
Owner: Send Manna Too LLC  
Classification: Internal Use  
Repository: Send-Manna-Too-LLC-Business-Governance  

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the development plan for Release 0.1 of the Send Manna Too LLC Business Governance Framework.

The purpose of Release 0.1 is to establish the governance foundation for how Send Manna Too LLC creates, manages, reviews, approves, and improves its business governance, commercial practices, advisory standards, and intellectual property.

## 2. Design Intent

This release is intentionally limited to foundation-level governance. It is not intended to produce operational templates, contracts, statements of work, proposal materials, pricing guides, or client-facing deliverables.

The goal is to establish a durable structure that supports trusted advisory work, consistent quality, professional independence, commercial integrity, and long-term relationship building.

## 3. Release Objectives

Release 0.1 will establish:

- The governing philosophy of Send Manna Too LLC.
- The document hierarchy for future governance artifacts.
- The quality standard for all future deliverables.
- The initial decision register.
- The future considerations register.
- The development and release discipline for future work.

## 4. Scope

Release 0.1 includes only the following deliverables:

1. Release 0.1 Development Plan.
2. Send Manna Too LLC Constitution.
3. Document Hierarchy Standard.
4. Document Quality Standard.
5. Decision Register.
6. Future Considerations Register.

## 5. Out of Scope

The following are intentionally deferred to later releases:

- Master Services Agreement.
- Statement of Work.
- Proposal standard.
- Pricing framework.
- Commercial conversation framework.
- Partner governance standard.
- Vendor evaluation standard.
- Referral and endorsement standard.
- Conflict of interest standard.
- Client testimonial and reference standard.
- AI usage and disclosure standard.
- Brand usage standard.

## 6. Development Principles

The framework will be developed according to these principles:

- Integrity before growth.
- Relationships before transactions.
- Evidence before opinion.
- Governance before execution.
- Quality before speed.
- Simplicity before complexity.
- Consistency before customization.
- Long-term trust before short-term revenue.
- Build once; reuse often.
- Continuous improvement without unnecessary expansion.

## 7. Quality Gates

Every Release 0.1 artifact must pass the following gates before approval:

- Accuracy review.
- Evidence review.
- Executive readability review.
- Spelling, grammar, and punctuation review.
- Formatting and consistency review.
- Objection and client-perspective review.
- Brand alignment review.
- Release readiness review.

## 8. Governance Hierarchy

The framework will use the following hierarchy:

1. Constitution.
2. Policies.
3. Standards.
4. Frameworks.
5. Procedures and playbooks.
6. Templates, checklists, and scripts.
7. Records.

Each document must have one primary purpose and must not duplicate guidance from another document.

## 9. Key Risks and Controls

| Risk | Control |
|---|---|
| Scope creep | Use the Future Considerations Register. |
| Duplicate guidance | Enforce the one-source-of-truth principle. |
| Unsupported claims | Require evidence or clearly labeled professional judgment. |
| Excessive complexity | Apply the material improvement rule. |
| Brand dilution | Use Send Manna Too LLC as the formal brand reference. |
| Inconsistent quality | Apply the Document Quality Standard. |
| Commercial misalignment | Trace commercial guidance to governing principles. |

## 10. Success Criteria

Release 0.1 is complete when:

- All in-scope artifacts are drafted, reviewed, and approved.
- The document hierarchy is established.
- The quality standard is approved.
- The decision register is active.
- The future considerations register is active.
- The release passes final shakeout review.
- The repository is clean and pushed to GitHub.

## 11. Release Strategy

Release 0.1 will be developed locally, committed to Git, and pushed to GitHub after validation.

Future releases will build on this foundation and should not bypass the approved hierarchy, quality gates, or decision process.

## 12. Approval

This document is approved when Send Manna Too LLC confirms that it accurately defines the purpose, scope, quality expectations, and success criteria for Release 0.1.
