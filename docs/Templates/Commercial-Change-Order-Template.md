## 1. Use Boundary

This template supports a written change to an authorized engagement.

A completed change order:

1. Is not effective until approved through applicable business, commercial, contractual, governance, and signature authority.
2. Does not authorize work merely because it was requested or drafted.
3. Requires qualified review when legal, insurance, tax, regulatory, privacy, security, IP, liability, or professional obligations are affected.
4. Shall not be stored in the public repository when it contains client identity, actual pricing, signatures, or confidential terms.

## 9. Effective-Date and Work-Control Rule

Affected work shall not begin until:

1. The change is approved.
2. Required signatures are complete.
3. Qualified review is complete.
4. Required payment or commercial conditions are satisfied.
5. The engagement record is updated.
6. The scope baseline is updated.
7. Required information and dependencies are available.
8. The DEC-0017 pilot boundary remains satisfied when applicable.

The prior approved scope remains controlling until these conditions are met.
