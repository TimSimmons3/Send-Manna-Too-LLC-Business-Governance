# Release 0.9 - Client Engagement Governance Policy Plan

## Objective

Create the Client Engagement Governance Policy as the second controlled governance policy within the Send Manna Too LLC Business Governance Framework.

## Scope

This release creates one policy only:

- docs/Foundation/Client-Engagement-Governance-Policy.md

This release also updates:

- CHANGELOG.md

## Policy Objectives

The policy shall define governance requirements for:

- Client qualification
- Engagement authorization
- Scope governance
- Assumption management
- Client communications
- Deliverable governance
- Approval authority
- Change governance
- Risk escalation
- Engagement closeout

## Out of Scope

This release shall not create or modify:

- Contracts
- Commercial terms
- Pricing guidance
- Confidentiality policy
- Records retention policy
- Third-party governance
- Publication procedures
- Governance architecture

## Validation Requirements

Before approval, validate:

- Document hierarchy alignment
- No duplicate governance
- ASCII compliance
- No trailing whitespace
- Markdown quality
- Clean git diff

## Deliverables

- docs/Foundation/Client-Engagement-Governance-Policy.md
- docs/Releases/Release-0.9-Client-Engagement-Governance-Policy-Plan.md
- docs/Releases/Release-0.9-Client-Engagement-Governance-Policy-Closeout.md
