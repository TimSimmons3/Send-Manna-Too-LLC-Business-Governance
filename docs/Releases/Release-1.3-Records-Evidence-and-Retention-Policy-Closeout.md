## Closeout Notes

The Records Evidence and Retention Policy establishes the governing baseline for record identification, evidence integrity, retention decisions, preservation, confidentiality, recoverability, review, authorized disposition, escalation, and exceptions.

Existing domain policies continue to define which records and evidence are required within their respective scopes. Release 1.3 governs the cross-cutting lifecycle expectations for those records.

The policy is intentionally governance-level and does not replace legal review, contractual requirements, client-specific obligations, approved retention schedules, technical records-management controls, or professional judgment.
